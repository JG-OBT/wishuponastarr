How It All Started

Our Story 📖

⭐ ✨ ⭐

Wish Upon a Starre was founded in 2017 by Abbie, who has a deep background in performing and a passion for creating magical memories. After working as a professional performer in theatres across the UK and with the prestigious Disney Cruise Line, Abbie returned home to Dorset with a clear mission: to bring theatre-quality, deeply magical character entertainment directly to local families.

What started as a single princess visiting local homes has blossomed into Dorset's premier, award-winning entertainment company, supporting a team of professionally trained performers and bringing smiles to thousands of little starres every single year.

Abbie • Founder & Lead Performer
🌸

Why we do it

Childhood is short, and the years of pure belief and wonder are even shorter. We believe that when a child meets their hero, it shouldn't just look like someone in a costume—it should feel like meeting the real character. We do this to capture that fleeting moment of absolute magic, helping families create precious memories that they will treasure for a lifetime.

✨

What makes us different

We stand apart because of our training and theatrical standards. Every single performer we hire has professional background in vocals, drama, or musical theatre. Our custom-made dresses are crafted from heavy, luxurious bridal satins (no cheap plastic-style fabrics!), and our custom wigs are styled beautifully. We bring live, highly skilled singing performances and engaging theatre to every single booking.

⭐⭐⭐

A little magic, delivered professionally

Behind the glitter and fairy dust lies a highly organised and fully insured small business. We make booking a breeze with clear, timely communication, transparent pricing, and 100% reliable scheduling. All our main characters are fully DBS-checked, public liability insured, and trained in active crowd engagement. We show up on time, handle the entertainment fully, and let parents sit back, relax, and enjoy the party!
