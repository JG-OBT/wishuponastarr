Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our scheduled sessions.

Do I need to book public sessions in advance? ▼

Yes, we strongly recommend booking in advance. Our Sensory and Song classes and holiday character workshops are extremely popular and have strict capacity limits to ensure a safe, high-quality experience for everyone.

Can I book you for private nurseries or school workshops? ▼

Absolutely! We regularly supply nurseries, preschools, and primary schools across Dorset with sensory song and educational workshops. Please reach out via email for block booking rates.

What happens if an event is canceled due to weather? ▼

In the extremely rare event of a cancellation, we will notify all ticket holders immediately via email/text and provide a full automatic refund or coordinate transfer tickets to the next scheduled date.
